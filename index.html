favorite baroque composer

My favorite baroque composer is Teleman.
I love him for his beautiful trumpet duets that will instantly transport you to a giant ballroom with giant crystal chandeliers where everyone is dressed to their finest.

favorite classic composer

My favorite composer from the classic era has to be Mozart.
I shouldn't need an explanation for this one but if you still want one go see his requiem live and youwill be incredibly moved.

favorite romantic composer

My favorite romantic composer is definetly Brahms.
Brahms' symphony number two is one of the most soothing pieces of music there is. The Brahms lullaby is very similar to a motif the cellos have in this piece so I could fall asleep to it anyday.

favorite modern composer

My favorite modern composer is Hindemith.
The Hindemith Trumpet Sonata is my favorite solo piece to play ever and I think that his "morgenmusik" is the best brass quintet you can get.
